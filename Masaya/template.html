Valid bet rules：

  1. Sportsbook valid bet calculation

    Odds must be equal or greater than 1.75 (EU) or 0.75 (Asian) to be counted as valid bet (will be calculated based on the betting amount). Lower than the odds mentioned above will not be counted as valid bet. Draw, win half, lose half will not be counted as valid bet as well, unless otherwise supported by the provider.

  2. Esports valid bet calculation

    Odds must be equal or greater than 1.70 (EU) or 0.70 (Asian) to be counted as valid bet (will be calculated based on the betting amount). Lower than the odds mentioned above will not be counted as valid bet. Draw, win half, lose half will not be counted as valid bet as well, unless otherwise supported by the provider.
